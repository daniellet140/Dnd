Features:

Savage Attacks: When I score a critical hit with a melee weapon attack, I can roll one of the weapon's damage dice one additional time and add it to the extra damage of the critical hit.
Relentless Endurance: When I am reduced to 0 hit points but not killed outright, I can drop to 1 hit point instead. I can't use this feature again until I finish a long rest.

Fighter 5 - Battle Master

Great Weapon Fighting: Reroll 1 or 2 on damage if wielding two-handed/versatile melee weapon in both hands
Second Wind: As a bonus action, I regain 1d10 + fighter level HP; I can use this once per short rest
Action Surge: Once per short rest, I can take one additional action on my turn on top of my normally allowed actions
Combat Superiority: I gain 4 superiority dice (d8) that I can use to fuel special Maneuvers. I regain all superiority dice after a short rest

  • Sweeping Attack: Use after hitting a creature and a second creature is within 5 ft of the first If the original attack roll hits this second creature, it takes the superiority die in damage
  • Riposte: When missed in melee, I can use my reaction to make one melee attack vs. the attacker. If the attack hits, I add the superiority die to my attack's damage
  • Maneuvering Attack: Use after hitting a creature; I add the superiority die to my attack's damage. An ally can use reaction to move half speed without opportunity attack from the target

Student of War: I have proficiency with one artisan's tool set of my choice - Carpenter's Tools

Barbarian 4 - Totem Warrior

Rage: Start/end as bonus action; add damage to melee weapons that use Str; lasts 1 min Adv. on Strength checks/saves (not attacks); resistance to bludgeoning/piercing/slashing Stops if I end turn without attacking or taking damage since last turn, or unconscious
Unarmored Defense: Without armor, my AC is 10 + Dexterity modifier + Constitution modifier + shield
Danger Sense: You have advantage on Dexterity Saving Throws against effects that you can see, such as traps and Spells. To gain this benefit, you can't be Blinded, Deafened, or Incapacitated.
Reckless Attack: You can throw aside all concern for defense to Attack with fierce desperation. When you make your first Attack on Your Turn, you can decide to Attack recklessly. Doing so gives you advantage on melee weapon Attack rolls using Strength during this turn, but Attack rolls against you have advantage until your next turn.
Spirit Seeker: I can cast Beast Sense and Speak with Animals as rituals
Bear Spirit: While raging, I have resistance to all damage types except psychic
